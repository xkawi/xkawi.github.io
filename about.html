About

A hands-on generalist and versatile software engineer with a passion for building impactful products and tools that solve real needs. I tend to take a big-picture first approach in solving technical problems, which allows me to double down on the exact root problem to fix.

Professionally, I have built software for a wide range of applications, from consumer-facing web, mobile apps, chatbots to internal tooling development, and recently, platform engineering. I have experience working in startups and scale-ups and enjoy every aspect of it, from collaborating with people from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, learning about the business domains, architecting the solutions, and being able to explore new ideas and technologies for the evolving requirements.

Outside engineering, I enjoy tinkering with hardware such as building split keyboards from scratch or custom mechanical keyboards, building my own PC, or anything that I can dismantle. Other than that, I try to keep up with the latest tech news and trends (e.g., AI), and maybe start writing about my learning and thoughts here more often too, so stay tuned!
